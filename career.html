Career Objective

Where I’m heading

I aim to build reliable, human‑centered computing systems that scale—from mobile apps that keep people connected to backend services that stay resilient under load. I’m especially interested in platforms that combine real‑time data, geospatial context, and AI‑driven decision making.

Near‑term, I want to deepen my expertise in Android, real‑time systems, and distributed backends, and contribute to a team that values testing, observability, and clean interfaces. Long‑term, I want to architect systems that balance performance with ethics—minimizing data movement, designing for privacy by default, and communicating trade‑offs transparently.
